Four fields, read honestly.

Field A · Medicine

AI × Brain & Neuroscience

The most clinically mature thread. Deep learning reads MRI and PET scans, and in 2025 the first Alzheimer's blood test was cleared. AI stays decision-support, not autonomous diagnosis.

Field B · Medicine

AI × Drug Discovery & TCM

Field C · Quantum

Quantum Computing × Molecular Prediction

The least mature, the most discussed. Today's noisy devices cannot yet model real drug molecules, and serious value sits in the 2030s.
